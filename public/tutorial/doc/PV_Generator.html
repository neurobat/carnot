PV generator
Path: CARNOT/Electricity

Purpose
Simulate a PV panel.

Description
This block simultes a pv panel and calculates the output of the module in Ws based on the parameters - Wp of one panel at STC [W] - temperature coefficient of Pmax [1/K] - number of panels [1]

Inputs:

  • weather inclined

Outputs:

  • DC power of the PV generator

 

Parameters and Dialog Box

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
