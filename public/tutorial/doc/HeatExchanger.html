heat exchanger
Path: CARNOT/heat_source

Purpose
Calculates the output THV of the hot and cold fluid of a heat exchanger.

Description
The heat exchanger is characterised by the number of transfer units (NTU). Input temperatures are delayed by the heat capacity of the exchanger. Parallel, cross and counter flow are possible.

      Thot_out = Thot_in - psi * (Thot_in - Tcold_in) (1)

      Tcold_out = Tcold_in - Whot/Wcold * (Thot_in - Thot_out) (2)

      ua = ua0 * ( (mdothot/mdot_nomhot)^ua_exphot * + (mdotcold/mdot_nomcold)^ua_expcold ) (3)

      NTU = (ua)/Whot (4)

      psi1 = exp(-NTU*(1 + min(w1,w2)/max(w1,w2)*(1 - 2*flow))) (5)

      psi = (1-psi) / (1+min(w1,w2)/max(w1,w2)*(1-flow*(1+ psi)))(6)

The delay for the inlet temperature is:

      (0.5*cap)*dT/dt = (Tin - T) - UAloss * (Tamb - T)

  • nominal heat transfer [W/K]

  • resistance coefficient for flow hot part
  • diameter cold part [m]
  • friction coefficient for cold part
  • heat loss to environment [W/K]
  • capacity [J/K]
  • initial temperature [°C]
