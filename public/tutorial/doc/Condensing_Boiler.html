Condensing_Boiler
Path: CARNOT/heat_source

Purpose
Simple model of a condensing boiler.

Description
The boiler model is a simple model which considers only the constant nominal power transfer on the combustion chamber side of the furnace. On the water side of the furnace, a multinode model is used to model the time dependent conditions. The differential equation for the water temperature

characteristic of a node in the furnace is   

(m*c/N)*dTNode/dt = U1/N*(Tamb-Tnode) + mdot*cf(Tin-Tnode)+Pact/N

the actual power is

Pact = eta_lookup_table * Pnom * ctrl
