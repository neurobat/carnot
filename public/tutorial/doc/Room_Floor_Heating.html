room floor heating
Path: CARNOT/loads

Purpose
Model of two rooms, separated by a wall. The rooms may be heated by a floor heating.

Description
The beneath and above laying room are modelled as "virtual zones" with a fixed temperature. For the description of the blocks, see THV_P_adapter, wall_out, floor_in, room_node.

Parameters and Dialog Box

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
States                           corresponding to the number of nodes
Vectorized                   No

 

Validation

  • The model has been compared to the results given by Feist (Feist 1994) and to measured data and calculated data of the SOPASIM-project (Balters 1995).
  • Comparison to measurements has also been done with the data form a project in Argentina (Mueller 1999).
  • A complete validation was done by FHNW in the work for IEA Task 32 and 44.
