Solar Position
Path: CARNOT/Basic/Weather_and_Sun

Purpose
Calculates the position of the sun according to the simulation time.

Description
The sun position is calculated in the S-function "sunpos". The values, that are not relevant for the calculation are passed through.

Parameters and Dialog Box

Outputs:
1 - solar zentith angle
2 - solar azimuth angle

Literature:
Duffie, J., Beckman, W.:Solar Engineering of thermal processes, John Wiley & Sons, Inc. 2006
Deutscher-Wetter-Dienst: Formeln für den Sonnenstand, 1990
Reindl, Beckman, Duffie: Diffuse Fraction Correlations, Solar Energy, 45, 1, 1990

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No

 

Validation

  • The function was compared with TRNSYS radiation processor (Type 16) and showed good congruence.
  • The sun position is regularily checked by files like meteonormminute2wformat and no difference is found
