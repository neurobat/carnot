fixed surface
Path: CARNOT/heat_source, CARNOT/weather

Purpose Model to describe a fixed collector position.


Description This model is used to define a fixed surface position, in particular for collector surfaces. The position is specified by three angles, the surface or collector slope respectively, the azimuth angle of the surface/collector and the angle of rotation around the longitudinal axis (usually the axis of a riser), each in degrees [°]. Output is the position vector [collector slope, collector azimuth, collector rotation]. The angles are shown in the following picture:




Figure: Collector position angles

The slope or incidence angle describes the inclination of the surface/collector to a horizontal surface, the azimuth angle the difference to the north-south direction and the rotation angle the difference to the east-west direction. The parameters are determined in the mask.

Parameters and Dialog Box

  • incidence angle (0 = horizontal, 90 = vertical) [degree]
  • azimuth angle (0° south, -90° east) [degree]
  • rotation angle (east direction negative)[degree]

Characteristics
