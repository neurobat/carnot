mix T mdot
Path: CARNOT/basics

Purpose
Calculation of mixed values of temperature and mass flow after a Tee-piece.

Description
The block calculates the values of temperature and mass flow rate after the reassembly of two flows streams in a Tee-piece. The mass flows are added, and the temperature is weighted according to the mass flow.

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
