ventilation
Path: CARNOT/loads/Room_node

Purpose
Model the ventilation of a room  for the simple building model.

Description
The block is a model for a ventilation flow rate in 3/s, flowing in a room.

The inputs are:

  • ambient temperature in °C
  • room temperature in °C

Output is:

  • the thermal loss or gain of the building due to the ventilation [W]

Parameters:
1) Air exchange rate in 1/h: A value of 1 means that the volume of the house is exchanged once per hour.
2) Heat recovery rate: Value between 0 and 1 to calculate the heat recovery. The value is used in the power balance.
3) The volume of the house.

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
