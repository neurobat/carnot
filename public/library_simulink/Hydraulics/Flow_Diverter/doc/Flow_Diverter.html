Flow diverter
Path: CARNOT/Hydraulic

Purpose
Divide the entering mass flow in two mass flows according to the fraction fdiv

Description
Divide the entering mass flow in two mass flows according to the fraction fdiv. All other properties are fed through.
THV1 : mdot(1) = fdiv * mdot(in)
THV2 : mdot(2) = (1-fdiv) * mdot(in)

Inputs

  • THV in
  • Flow diversion rate fdiv

Outputs

  • THV 1
  • THV 2

 

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
