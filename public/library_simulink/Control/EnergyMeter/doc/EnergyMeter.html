energy meter
Path: CARNOT/outputs

Purpose
The block shows the power and energy gain or loss between inlet and outlet of a component.

Description
The block shows the power [W] and energy [J] gain or loss in one Thermo-Hydraulic Vector between the inlet and the outlet of one component. Connect THV1 to the vector entering the component and THV2 to the vector at the outlet.

NOTE: In SIMULINK version 2.0 scopes do not open when starting the simulation. You have to open the subsystem and double-click on the scope to see the graph.

Inputs:

  • Thermo-Hydraulic Vector at component inlet
  • Thermo-Hydraulic Vector at component outlet

Outputs:

  • power in [W]
  • energy in [J]

Thermo-Hydraulic Vector see storage demos of CARNOT

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
