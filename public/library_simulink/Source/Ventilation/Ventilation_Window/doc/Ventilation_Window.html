ventilation window
Path: CARNOT/source/ventilation

Purpose
Model the ventilation of a room  based on a standard air exchange rate and an additional opening of windows. The model is suitable for the simple building model.

Description
The block is a model for a ventilation flow rate in 3/s, flowing in a room.

Opening of the window simulates the free driven ventilation through a window with an opening angle of [0,1]* max. angle. Valid for angles between 8° and 180°. Output is the VOLUME FLOW RATE in m^3/h. Calculation based on the Ansatz from Weber.

The inputs are:

  • ambient temperature in °C
  • room temperature in °C

Output is:

  • the thermal loss or gain of the building due to the ventilation [W]

Parameters:
1) Air exchange rate in 1/h: A value of 1 means that the volume of the house is exchanged once per hour.
2) Heat recovery rate: Value between 0 and 1 to calculate the heat recovery. The value is used in the power balance.
3) The volume of the house.

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
