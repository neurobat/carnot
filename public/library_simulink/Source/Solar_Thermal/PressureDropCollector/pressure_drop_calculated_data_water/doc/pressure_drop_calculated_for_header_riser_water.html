pressure drop calculated for header riser fluid:water
Path: CARNOT/heat_source/pressure_drop of collector

Purpose
This block calculates the pressure drop for the usual water header-riser.

Description
This block interpolates the pressure drop of a collector for a usual header riser- arrangement. The number of risers (= number of absorber fins) is 10 per 1 m of header. The inner diameter of the riser is 6 mm, of the header 20 mm. Fluid in the collector is water. An equation of Dunkle and Davey ("Flow distribution in absorber banks", ISES Conference, 1970) is used. See colpdrop in the manual or type

 help colpdrop

on the command line for more information. Inputs:

  • temperature [°C]
  • mass flow [kg/s]

Outputs:

  • pressure drop of the collector (positive value) [Pa]

Parameters and Dialog Box

  • surface of one collector [m2]
  • total surface of array [m2] (NOT TO BE CHANGED IN THIS MODULE)

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
