Pressure drop quadratic fitted data fluid:water
Path: CARNOT/heat_source/pressure_drop of collector

Purpose
This block calculates the pressure drop of a collector on the basis of measured data.

Description
This block calculates the pressure drop of a collector from a fitted parabolic curve. The only difference to the block "pressure drop quadratic fitted data fluid water" is that in the current block there is no temperature correction (see look-up table "T-correction" under the mask). User may specify their own temperature correction in the look-up table.
Give parameters in SI-units.
Multiply with 3.6e5 to convert from [mbar/(liter/hour)] to [Pa/(kg/s)]
Multiply with 1.296e+9 to convert form [mbar/(liter/hour)2] to [Pa/(kg/s)2]".

Inputs:

  • temperature [°C]
  • mass flow [kg/s]
  • pressure [Pa]
  • lin_qua (linear and quadratic pressure drop coefficients)

Outputs:

  • pressure drop of the collector (positive value) [Pa]
  • linear and quadratic pressure drop coefficients

Parameters and Dialog Box

  • linear term of pressure drop [Pa/(kg/s)]
  • quadratic term of pressure drop [Pa/(kg/s)2]
  • surface of one collector [m2]
  • total surface of array [m2] (NOT TO BE CHANGED IN THIS MODULE)

Characteristics

Sample time                Inherited from driving block
