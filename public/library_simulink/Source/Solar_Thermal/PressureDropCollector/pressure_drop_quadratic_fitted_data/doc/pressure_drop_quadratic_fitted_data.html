pressure drop quadratic fitted data
Path: CARNOT/heat_source/pressure_drop of collector

Purpose
This block calculates the pressure drop of a collector on the basis of quadratic fitted data.

Description
This block calculates the pressure drop of a collector from a fitted parabolic curve. Other temperatures than at test condition are not corrected. Give parameters in SI-units.

Multiply with 3.6e5 to convert from [mbar/(l/h)] to [Pa/(kg/s)].

Multiply with 1.296e9 to convert from [mbar/(l/h)2] to [Pa/(kg/s)2]. Set "surface of one collector" to the surface of one collector for a parallel arrangement of collectors. Set it to the surface of the array "acol" to model a series arrangement.

Inputs:

  • temperature [°C]
  • mass flow [kg/s]
  • pressure [Pa]
  • lin_qua (linear and quadratic pressure drop coefficients)

Outputs:

  • pressure drop of the collector (positive value) [Pa]
  • linear and quadratic pressure drop coefficients

Parameters and Dialog Box

  • linear term of pressure drop [Pa/(kg/s)]
  • quadratic term of pressure drop [Pa/(kg/s)2]
  • surface of one collector [m2]
  • total surface of array [m2] (NOT TO BE CHANGED IN THIS MODULE)

Characteristics

Sample time                Inherited from driving block
