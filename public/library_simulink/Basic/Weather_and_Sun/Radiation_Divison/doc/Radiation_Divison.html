radiation division
Path: CARNOT/weather

Purpose
Calculates the direct and diffusive radiation on the horizontal surface as parts of the global radiation and the sun position.

Description
The direct and diffusive radiation are calculated by the formulas of Duffie/Beckman, 1991. The model calculates the sun_position described by the three sun-angles zenith-angle, azimuth-angle and the angle between collector normal and the sun. The calculation is carried out based the Orgill and Hollands model (1977).
This block is also available as workspace function radiationdivision.

Inputs:

  • time : real time or simulation time [s]
  • Iglob : global radiation on horizontal surface (W/m²)

Outputs:

  • Idir : direct radiation (W/m²)
  • Idfu : diffusive radiation (W/m²)
  • SunAngle
  • Zenith
  • Azimuth

Literature: Duffie, J., Beckman, W.:Solar Engineering of thermal processes, John Wiley & Sons, Inc. 2006
