solar hour angle
Path: CARNOT/basics

Purpose
Calculates the solar hour angle in degree. The hour angle is 0° at solar noon (sun is at azimuth 0° or 180°). The angle is negative in the morning and positive in the evening. At sunrise / sunset the angle is -90° / 90°.

Description

The block calls the function "solar_hour_angle" in the CARNOT Library Carlib where the calculation is effectuated. The equation was taken from Duffie, Beckmann: Solar Engineering of Thermal Processes, 2006.

 

Inputs:

  • time [s]

January 1st 0:00:00 = 0 s
December 31st 24:00:00 = 365*24*3600 s

Characteristics
