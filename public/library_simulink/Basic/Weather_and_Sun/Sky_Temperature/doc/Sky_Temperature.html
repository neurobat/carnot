sky temperature
Path: CARNOT/weather

Purpose
Calculates the skytemperature.

Description
When there is the index of cloudness, the sky temperature is calculated by the formula of BERDAHL. Otherwise the sky temperature is calculated by the formula of UNSWORTH.
This block is also available as workspace function skytemperature.

Inputs:

  • tamb : ambient temperature (°C)
  • hum : humidity (%) [0;100]
  • cloud : cloudindex [0;1]

Outputs:

  • tsky : sky temperature (°C)

Literature: Duffie, J., Beckman, W.: Solar Engineering of thermal processes, John Wiley & Sons, Inc. 2006
