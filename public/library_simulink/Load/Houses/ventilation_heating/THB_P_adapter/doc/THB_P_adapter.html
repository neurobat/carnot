THV_P_ adapter
Path: CARNOT/misc

Purpose
Adapt active components to the house-model, i.e. to model a floor heating.

Description
The model consists of a fluid mass, heated (or cooled) by the incoming flow. The power is than transferred with the heat transfer coefficient and the temperature difference to the heating power for a wall or floor.

Parameters and Dialog Box

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
States                           1
Vectorized                   No
